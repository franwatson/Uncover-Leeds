Nights Out In Leeds

Click on the pictures to find out more about each place

Be At One££
Be at One is a lively bar known for selling exellent cocktails and offer mixing masterclasses. There are two of these bars in Leeds. Located Millennium Square, 6, Great George St, Leeds LS2 3AD and 19 Boar Ln, Leeds LS1 6EA. This bar has a great, inviting, casual atmosphere. Make sure you head down for their happy hour to get two for one on cocktails however if you don't fancy a cocktail, they also do beers, wines and spirits and delicious mocktails.

The Alchemist£££
The Alchemist is a very pricy cocktail bar but also very popular as it is a unique experience. The Alchemist describes itself as "masters in the dark arts of molecular mixology and demons in the kitchen... mixologists create every cocktail with an obsessive eye for detail, presented in vessels orchestrated to add a devilish dash of theatre, they bedazzle, bewitch and set the scene for everything we do". Located Yorkshire House, Greek St, Leeds LS1 5SH.

Las Iguanas££
This place is a personal favouite venue to go to, not for the food, despite it being a chain resturant, but for the cocktail bar, always with two for one on their delicious cocktails. The location is also great before a night out on Call Lane which is just round the corner. Location Unit 3 Cloth Hall St, Leeds LS1 2HD.

The Hedley Verity£
The Headley Verity is a classic Spoons pub. Cheap food and drinks, therefore a fantastic night out in Leeds. There are multiple Wetherspoons in the center of Leeds. There is one in the train station (North Concourse, Leeds City Station, Leeds LS1 4DY), Becketts Bank (28-30 Park Row, Leeds LS1 5HU), The Cuthbert Brodrick (99 Portland Cres, Leeds LS1 3HJ) and The Headley Verity (43a, Woodhouse Ln, Leeds LS1 3HQ) however the way in which Headley Verity differs from the others is because after a certain time, aprox 9:30pm, the classic pub dims the lights and becomes a night club with loud music and a dancefloor.
