Events

Leeds Christmas Markets

9 Nov - 22 Dec

Christmas Markets are lots of fun for the entire family, with fairground rides, food stalls and German beer for mummy and daddy to enjoy. Entry is free. Open from 10:30am–9:30pm, 7 days a week @ Millennium Square, Leeds LS2 3AD

Outdoor Cinema

Harewood House, Leeds, West Yorkshire, LS17 9LG

The outdoor cinema is a treat for the summer months. The Luna Cinema presents three classic movies, including Harry Potter and the Philosopher's Stone, Pretty Woman and The Greatest Showman, for a night under the stars. Tickets are £15 per adult and £10 per child. Visit thelunacinema.com for more details.

Leeds Gay Pride

5 August 2018

Leeds Pride is an annual Gay Pride celebration of lesbian, gay, bisexual and trans life held in the city of Leeds. The main event on the Sunday will start with 2 hours of entertainment in Millennium Square, followed by a parade through the city centre with over 60 floats and 5 hours of entertainment on Lower Briggate to finish off an incredible weekend.

Leeds Festival

23-25 August 2019

Leeds Festival takes place in Bramham Park. Weekend, camping, glamping and day tickets are all available to purchase at a range of prices with children under 13 able to attend for free. See leedsfestival.com for the line up and purchasing details.
